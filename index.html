We spin tracks inspired by the rhythm of the city. From the roar of the subway to the whispers of late-night rooftops, our mixes bring the energy of New York’s underground to your next pregame, gathering, or moment of creative flow.

About 11th St. Sounds

We’re a collective of DJs and music lovers who channel the pulse of New York City’s underbelly into every set.

Our mission: to bring sonic adventures that transcend boundaries. House, techno, hip-hop breaks, disco edits—anything goes, as long as it moves you. Just like riding the train, every journey is unique, unexpected, and alive.
